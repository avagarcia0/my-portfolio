One cool concept in social choice theory is Voter Satisfaction Efficiency, or VSE for short. VSE is intended to act as a holistic measure of how well a voting method does at satisfying voter's preferences. However, because it is hard to get data from real-life elections and even harder to read people's minds to get their preferences, VSE is measured through simulated elections.
